# 🚀 JobNexus – Smart Job Portal

> **Connecting talent with opportunity.** A modern full-stack platform that streamlines the hiring process by connecting **Job Seekers**, **Employers**, and **Administrators** on a unified platform.

🌐 **Live Demo:** https://jobnexus-lzf7.onrender.com/

> **Note:** The application is hosted on Render. The first request may take **30–60 seconds** to load if the server has been inactive.

---

## 🌟 Overview

**JobNexus** is a modern full-stack job portal designed to simplify recruitment by providing a seamless experience for job seekers, employers, and administrators.

The platform enables candidates to discover and apply for jobs, helps employers manage recruitment efficiently, and provides administrators with tools to maintain platform integrity.

🚀 **JobNexus is actively maintained, with new features and UI improvements being added regularly.**

---

# 💼 Three Worlds, One Platform

## 👨‍💼 Job Seekers

Find your next opportunity with ease.

* 🔍 Browse and discover jobs
* ⚙️ Search and filter jobs by title, location, experience, job type, and salary
* 📄 View detailed job descriptions
* 🎯 Apply for jobs directly
* 📋 Track application history

---

## 🏢 Employers

Manage recruitment from one dashboard.

* 👥 Create and manage company profiles
* ✍️ Post and manage job openings
* 📊 Monitor hiring analytics
* 📈 Track candidates through the hiring pipeline
* 📅 Manage interview schedules

---

## 🛡️ Administrators

Maintain and manage the platform.

* 🔐 Secure admin login
* ✅ Approve or reject company registration requests before job posting
* 👥 Manage platform access and permissions
* 📊 Monitor platform activities
